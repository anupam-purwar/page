Anupam Purwar


My first research work was the result of my interest in energy conservation and I experimented with classical control systems techniques,Fuzzy logic and eventually Artificial Neural Networks. This resulted into my 1st research paper during 2nd year of my undergraduate studies at BITS Pilani. This paper was received very well and won best paper award in IEEE Indicon 2014. This triggered my interest in research and applied Artificial Intelligence motivating me to join Indian Institute of Science Bangalore. At IISc, I was able to recieve research grants from GoI and forge collabortions with IIT-Kanpur, IIT-Roorkee and ISRO which facilitated my research in computational design, experimental material processing and led to 5 journal publications. After a wonderful stint at IISc, I pursued MBA from Indian School of Business, got my first flavour of finance from some of the best professors there like Prof. Bhagwan and was greatly influenced by Prof.Sanjiv Das's course on FinTech. Post ISB, I took up a Management role with Amazon but gradually moved into Research Science and worked with Amazon, Seattle for ~6.5 years as a Sr. Research Scientist in a Global Science Team. Currently, I lead Generative AI initiatives for Sprinklr Inc. (NYSE: CXM) as the Director of Machine Learning. I am passionate about research and even more passionate about teaching, interacting with curious minds.

Short Bio

Anupam Purwar lead Generative AI initiatives for Sprinklr Inc. (NYSE: CXM) as the Director of Machine Learning leading their Agentic AI Charter. Prior to this, Anupam was a Sr. Research Scientist with Amazon for ~6.5 years where he led the development of data science and machine learning-based solutions for Amazon Global Fulfillment out of their Seattle office. Anupam holds an MBA in Finance and Information Systems from the Indian School of Business (ISB) and a Bachelor of Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani. He received merit scholarship and graduated among Top 5% in class both at ISB and BITS-Pilani. Prior to this, Anupam worked as a Scientist at Indian Institute of Science (IISc). At IISc, he was part of a multi-institutional effort which included IITs and ISRO to develop novel structures. Besides, he has authored 30+ peer reviewed articles pertaining to Generative AI, Machine Learning, IoT, computational design with 330+ citations and received multiple best paper awards. He is a certified Machine learning professional with 12+ certifications from Google and AWS. You can know more about him here by talking to this : Gen AI bot .

Anupam Purwar loves teaching, mentoring students, startups and has delivered talks on Generative AI, Machine Learning and career development at BITS Pilani, IIT Delhi and Indian School of Business. Most recently Anupam taught Agentic AI to a batch of 70 students in Seattle/Bellevue in a 10 week long programme . He is also one of the youngest receipient of Malviya Award from Indian Ceramic Society.
